How to Use InvoiceKit

How to Use InvoiceKit

Create, manage and download professional GST invoices — all stored on your device.

1

Set Up Your Company

Fill in your business details in the Your Company panel. This info appears on every invoice you generate.

  • Upload your company logo and signature image
  • Enter name, email, phone and address
  • Add your GST number
  • Fill in bank account details for payment
  • Add UPI ID and upload a QR code image
  • Tap Save Company when done
2

Create a New Invoice

Fill out the New Invoice form with your client's details and billing date.

  • Client name (required), email, phone and address
  • Phone must include country code (e.g. 919876543210)
  • Invoice number is assigned automatically
  • Use Reset Bill to clear the form at any time
3

Add Line Items

Tap + Add Line Item for each product or service. Fill in the details per row.

  • Description — what you are billing for
  • Transaction ID — optional reference number
  • Quantity and Unit Price in ₹
  • GST % — tax rate per item (e.g. 18)
  • Amount calculates automatically
  • Add a shipping charge below the item list if needed
4

Set Status and Save

Choose Unpaid or Paid status, then tap Save Invoice. The invoice is stored on your device and appears in the list below.

  • GST is split into CGST and SGST automatically
  • Invoice number increments with each save
  • Earnings summary updates instantly

1. Select Unpaid — saves as a Bill, for amounts not yet received from the client.
2. Select Paid — saves as an Invoice, for amounts already collected from the client.

5

Manage Saved Invoices

Each saved invoice has a row of action buttons for quick management.

  • Unpaid / Paid — toggle payment status instantly
  • View — open a full summary with all items and GST breakdown
  • Edit — load invoice back into the form to make changes
  • PDF — download a professional branded PDF
  • Share — PDF downloads then WhatsApp opens to client number
  • Delete — permanently remove the invoice
6

Download PDF Invoice

Tap PDF on any saved invoice to generate and download a complete PDF with your branding.

  • Includes your logo, company details and signature
  • Full item table with GST breakdown (CGST + SGST)
  • Bank details and QR code on unpaid invoices
  • Auto page breaks for long invoices
  • File named as Invoice No. + Client Name
7

Share via WhatsApp

Tap Share on any invoice — the PDF downloads first, then WhatsApp opens to your client's number so you can attach and send it.

  • Client phone number must include country code
  • Attach the downloaded PDF manually in WhatsApp
8

Track Earnings

The earnings bar above your saved invoices shows a live summary of all billing activity.

  • Total earnings across all invoices
  • Paid amount updates when you mark invoices
  • Invoice count shown in the section header

Pro Tips

💾

Download a PDF copy of every invoice right away — browser data can be cleared unexpectedly and invoices cannot be recovered.

📱

On mobile, the company settings appear above the invoice form — scroll up to access them. All buttons are touch-optimised.

🔢

Invoice numbers auto-increment with every save. Resetting the bill form does not reset the invoice counter.

🌐

All data is stored on this device only. Use the same browser to access your invoices. Clearing browser cache will erase all data.

About InvoiceKit

Last Updated: March 29, 2026

InvoiceKit is a simple, fast, and user-friendly web application designed to help individuals and businesses create professional invoices easily — with no installation, no account setup, and no complexity.

Purpose

The goal of this app is to provide a lightweight, easy-to-use billing solution that works entirely in your browser. No sign-up required. No software to install.

How It Works

  • All data is processed directly in your browser
  • No backend or server is used at any point
  • Your data stays on your device at all times

Features

  • Create and manage professional GST invoices
  • Auto calculation of subtotal, CGST, SGST and total
  • Download invoices as branded PDF files
  • Save invoices locally with payment status tracking
  • Share invoices directly via WhatsApp

Privacy First

InvoiceKit does not collect or store your data on any external server. Everything — your company details, bank information, client data, and invoices — remains completely private and under your full control on your own device.

Built For

  • Freelancers and independent consultants
  • Small businesses and traders
  • Students and individuals
  • Anyone who needs a quick, clean invoice

Vision

To create a simple, accessible invoicing tool that anyone can use without technical knowledge — fast, free, and completely private.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how InvoiceKit ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information. This application is designed as a frontend-only tool and does not collect or store personal data on external servers.

1. Information We Collect

Any information you enter — such as company details, bank account information, UPI ID, GST number, client information, and invoice items — is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. We do not transmit or store this data on any external server.

2. How Your Data Is Used

Your data is used only for:

  • Generating and downloading invoice PDFs
  • Auto-filling your company details on new invoices
  • Displaying saved invoices and earnings within the app

All processing happens locally in your browser.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored on your device using browser localStorage. This means:

  • Data remains on your device only
  • Data may be lost if you clear your browser storage
  • Data is not accessible from other devices or browsers

4. Data Sharing

We do not:

  • Sell your data to any party
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Upload your data to any server

5. Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you are responsible for the security of your device and browser. We do not have access to your stored data at any time.

6. Your Control

You have full control over your data:

  • Use the Reset button in Company Settings to clear company details
  • Delete individual invoices using the Delete button in the app
  • Clear all app data via your browser's site data or storage settings

7. Cookies & Tracking

InvoiceKit uses no cookies, no analytics, no advertising scripts, and no third-party tracking of any kind.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected with a new "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us at:

invoicekit.generate@gmail.com

Note: This application operates entirely on the client side and does not store user data on any external servers.
