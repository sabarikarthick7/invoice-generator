How to Use InvoiceKit

Get Started in Minutes 🚀

Follow these simple steps to create, manage & download professional invoices instantly.

1
🏢

Set Up Your Company

Find the Your Company panel on the left (desktop) or at the top of the page (mobile). Fill in your business details and tap Save Company.

  • Upload your company logo
  • Enter name, email & phone
  • Add your business address
  • Enter your GST number
  • Upload your signature image
2
👤

Add Client Details

In the New Invoice section, enter your client's information for the bill.

  • Client name (required)
  • Email address
  • Phone with country code (e.g. 919876543210)
  • Full billing address
  • Select the invoice date
3
📋

Add Line Items

Tap + Add Line Item for each product or service you're billing for.

  • Description — what you're billing
  • Transaction ID — optional reference
  • Quantity — number of units
  • Unit Price — price per item in ₹
  • GST % — tax rate (e.g. 18)
  • Amount calculates automatically
4
💾

Save the Invoice

Review totals (Subtotal, CGST, SGST, Total) and tap Save Invoice to store it locally.

  • Invoice number auto-increments
  • GST split into CGST + SGST
  • Appears in Saved Invoices immediately
5
⬇️

Download PDF

Tap ⬇ PDF on any saved invoice to download a professional PDF with your branding.

  • Includes your logo & signature
  • Full item table with GST breakdown
  • Auto page breaks for long invoices
  • Named as Invoice No. + Client Name
6
📤

Share via WhatsApp

Tap 📤 Share — the PDF downloads then WhatsApp opens to your client's number.

  • Client phone must include country code
  • PDF downloads first automatically
  • Manually attach it in WhatsApp
7
✅

Track Payment Status

Tap the Paid badge on any invoice to toggle its payment status.

  • Green badge = Paid
  • Earnings bar updates instantly
8
👁

View Invoice Details

Tap 👁 View for a full summary with all items, GST breakdown, and client info.

  • Download PDF directly from modal
  • See client address & email
  • View payment status at a glance

💡 Pro Tips

💾

Download a PDF copy of every invoice right away — browser data can be cleared unexpectedly.

📱

On mobile, your company settings panel appears above the invoice form — scroll up to access it anytime.

🔢

Invoice numbers auto-increment. Reset Bill only clears the form — it won't reset the counter.

🌐

All data is stored on this device only. Use the same browser to access your invoices again.
